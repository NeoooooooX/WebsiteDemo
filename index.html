I’m a beginner. Can i use it?

・Absolutely. The system interface is intuitive and well-structured, allowing even beginners to get started quickly. We also provide beginner guides, tutorial videos, and real-time customer support.

Do I need to install any software?

・No installation is required. All functions run entirely on the TradingView cloud, so you can start using it immediately after login.
・TradingView fully supports PC, Mac, and mobile devices — once you sign in with your authorized account, your settings and signals stay synchronized across all devices anytime, anywhere.

Which markets do your indicators support?

・They work on almost all major global markets — including stocks, cryptocurrencies, forex, futures, and commodities.
・As long as it’s available on a TradingView chart, you can use our indicators on it.

Will the tool automatically alert me to buy or sell signals?

・Yes. Each indicator includes a built-in real-time alert feature.
・When market conditions meet the signal criteria, you’ll receive an instant notification — no need to stare at the charts all day.

Are the indicator signals accurate?

・MatrixQuant indicators are not designed to predict the future — they are built to help you capture market rhythm faster, see structure clearly, filter out noise, and reduce emotional trading.

Does it support automated trading?

・We provide partially automated strategy modules for backtesting, signal triggering, and trade execution.
・However, strategy parameters should be fine-tuned manually according to your target market and timeframe for optimal results.

Can I upgrade or request a refund after subscribing?

・Upgrade: You can upgrade anytime by paying the price difference.
・Refund: Monthly plans are eligible for a full refund within 7 days.
・Annual plans are offered at a discounted rate and therefore non-refundable.
